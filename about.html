About EBED'S_HUB

Your story is our story - built by students, for students

Our Story

EBED'S_HUB was born out of a simple observation: GCTU students deserved access to quality cosmetics and fashion products without breaking their budgets. As fellow students, we understood the struggle of wanting to look good while managing limited resources.

What started as a small initiative to help classmates access affordable beauty products has grown into GCTU's most trusted campus marketplace for cosmetics and fashion. We're not just a business - we're part of the GCTU family.

Our Mission

To provide GCTU students with access to high-quality, affordable cosmetics and fashion products while building a supportive campus community.

Our Vision

To be the leading student-centered marketplace in Ghana, empowering young people to express themselves confidently through beauty and fashion.
