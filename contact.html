Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do you offer student discounts?

Yes! All GCTU students receive automatic discounts on our products. Just provide your student ID when shopping with us. Additional discounts are available during special campus events.

How does campus delivery work?

We offer free delivery within GCTU campus for orders above GHâ‚µ50. Orders are typically delivered within 2-3 hours during business hours. We'll contact you when your order is ready for delivery.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept mobile money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), bank transfers, and cash on delivery for campus orders. Student payment plans are also available for larger purchases.

Can I return or exchange products?

Yes, we have a 7-day return policy for unused cosmetics and a 14-day policy for fashion items. Products must be in original condition with receipt. Exchanges are welcome for different sizes or shades.

Do you have a physical store on campus?

We operate primarily online and through campus delivery, but we set up pop-up shops during special events and have a pickup location near the main library. Follow our social media for pop-up announcements!

How can I become a campus representative?

We're always looking for enthusiastic GCTU students to join our team! Campus reps earn commission on sales and get exclusive access to new products. Contact us for more information about opportunities.
